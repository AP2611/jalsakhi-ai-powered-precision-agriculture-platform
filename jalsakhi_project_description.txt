description: JalSakhi is an open-source, AI-powered precision agriculture platform designed to optimize water management for farmers and village administrators in India. The platform features a cross-platform mobile app (React Native + Expo), a Node.js/Express backend, and Python FastAPI-based ML microservices for crop water prediction, soil moisture forecasting, village water allocation, and an AI chatbot. JalSakhi supports multilingual interfaces (English, Hindi, Marathi), role-based access, real-time weather, analytics, and a collaborative open-source community.,
